Privacy Policy

Application: Callfit  |  Last updated: May 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Callfit ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application ("App"). By using Callfit, you agree to the terms of this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide the App's features:

  • Photos and Camera: When you use the food analysis feature, the App accesses your camera or photo library to capture or select a food image. Images are sent to Google's Gemini API for analysis and are not stored on our servers.
  • Body Data (Age, Height, Weight): If you use the personalized calorie plan feature, you may enter your age, height, weight, and activity level. This data is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to us.
  • Purchase Information: Subscription purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store your payment details, credit card numbers, or billing information.
  • Usage Preferences: Settings such as selected language and remaining free trial count are stored locally on your device via UserDefaults.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To analyze food photos and return calorie/nutrition estimates via the Gemini API.
  • To calculate a personalized daily calorie target based on data you voluntarily enter.
  • To manage your subscription status through Apple's StoreKit framework.
  • To remember your language preference and app settings between sessions.

3. Third-Party Services

Callfit uses the following third-party service:

  • Google Gemini API: Food images are transmitted to Google's Gemini API for AI-based food recognition. Google's use of this data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. We do not share any personally identifiable information with Google beyond the image content.
  • Apple App Store / StoreKit: All subscription billing is handled by Apple. We do not have access to your payment details. Apple's privacy practices are described at apple.com/legal/privacy.

4. Data Storage and Security

We do not operate servers that store your personal data. All personal information (body metrics, settings, trial count) is stored locally on your device. Food images sent to the Gemini API are used solely for real-time analysis and are not retained by us.

We take reasonable measures to protect information within our control, but no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Children's Privacy

Callfit is not directed to children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: You may request a copy of any personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: You may request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: You may request deletion of your personal data.
  • Portability: You may request your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Objection: You may object to our processing of your data.

Since most data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by uninstalling the App. For any other requests, contact us at the email below.

7. Data Retention

Local data (settings, body metrics, trial count) is retained on your device until you uninstall the App. Food images are not stored — they are processed in real time and discarded. Subscription status is verified through Apple and not stored independently by us.

8. Cookies and Tracking

Callfit is a native iOS application. We do not use cookies, web beacons, pixels, or any browser-based tracking technologies.

9. Subscriptions and Recurring Payments

Callfit offers a monthly auto-renewing subscription ("Callfit Pro") processed through Apple's App Store. Your subscription will automatically renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current billing period. You can manage and cancel your subscription in your Apple ID account settings. We do not have access to your billing details.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
